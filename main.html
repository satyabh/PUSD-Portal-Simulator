Student Portal Simulator

This project is just a prototype. Expect errors from incorrect text entries and arithmetic exceptions. I am still working on adding buttons to remove elements; so for now if you mess up on entering something; please refresh the page. Do not try to simulate with an empty category

This simulation is for students who wish to see their estimated grades because their teachers don't update things online. The system takes into account of weightage of each category. Begin by starting a category, (like tests, quizes, hw). Then enter assignments and pick a category to add to. Click Simulate when done
